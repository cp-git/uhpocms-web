Overview

University of Houston is a public institution that was founded in 1927. It has a total undergraduate enrollment of 38,581 (fall 2021), its setting is urban, and the campus size is 895 acres. It utilizes a semester-based academic calendar. University of Houston's ranking in the 2022-2023 edition of Best Colleges is National Universities, #182.
